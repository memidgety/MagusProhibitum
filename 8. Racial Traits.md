## Core Races

### Humans
___
**Tough as Nails** 4-Track Trait
You’re a natural survivor. Rolls made to heal an injury you're suffering from treat partial successes as full successes.

**Strong Stomach** 3-Track Trait
Reduce the impact of poisons, diseases and sickness.

**Ironclad Mind** 4-Track Trait
You are immune to hallucinations, mesmerics and mental compulsions

**Quick to Learn** 2-Track Trait
Humans pick up new techniques fast. Once per session, gain +1d on a roll involving a skill you have not previously used this session, or increase your impact of a unfamiliar task by one step.

**Jack of All Trades** 2-Track Trait
Humans are rarely the best at anything, but they’re rarely helpless. When rolling a skill you have no rating in, treat it as if you had 1. You may not use this trait on the same skill again that scene.

**Polyglot** 4-Track Trait
Mark to gain conversational knowledge of a language.  This knowledge remains until you
choose to mark and replace it with a new language.

**Resourceful** 3-Track Trait
Given scraps, time, or chaos, humans can make things work. Gain +1d when improvising tools, makeshift solutions, or unorthodox plans. You can produce a simple, mundane item once per session even if you lack the supplies.

**Open Hands, Open Heart** 3-Track Trait
Whenever you or your crew successfully defuse a situation that was heading toward a fight, clear a mark of [[5. Mires & Trauma|mire]].

**Unremarkable** 2-Track Trait
Sometimes the greatest power is blending in. Humans are forgettable enough that they get +1d to avoid notice in crowds, pass as locals, or deflect suspicion.

**Culturewise** 3-Track Trait
Humans bridge cultural, magical, and social divides. Humans are accepted almost anywhere as “common ground.”  Gain +1d to social rolls with new factions, strange beings, or unfamiliar cultures.

### Elves
___
**Elven Grace** 4-Track Trait
Naturally lithe and agile, elves are hard to land a hit on. Never take a cut when acting with agility, balance, or effortless motion - leaping, dancing, slipping through danger, or performing fine movements.

**Keen Senses** 3-Track Trait
Elves perceive what others overlook. When detecting danger, noticing subtle details, or reacting to sudden changes treat partial successes as full successes.

**Whisperstep** 2-Track Trait
Tread lightly, leaving almost no trace. When moving quietly, crossing natural terrain, or slipping past attention, you gain +1d. You may push yourself to vanish from view in a swirl of foliage, shadow, or starlight - gaining a brief window of perfect concealment.

**Timeworn Resilience** 3-Track Trait
Long lifespans have taught elves how to outlast hardship. Clear 1 more mire during [[11. Long Rests|Long Rests]].

**Enduring Perspective** 3-Track Trait
Long life breeds patience. Gain +1d on actions requiring multi-step planning. You may also assist an ally in a plan dealing with timed elements without marking mire.

**I Used to Know This** 3-Track Trait
Elves may pick up a hobby and drop it in their long life. Treat a partial success as a full success when rolling for an action without a skill adding to your dice pool (once per scene).


### Dwarves
___
**Stoutly Body** 5-Track Trait
Dwarves are built with compact power, dense muscle, and a frame shaped by generations spent in stone halls. Once per job you may lower the damage you take by 2.

**Stonecunning** 3-Track Trait
You possess an uncanny awareness of vibration, echo, and spatial structure. Gain +1d when Surveying enclosed spaces, underground chambers, stone-hewn corridors, or any type of masonry. You can sense movement through walls or floors as a subtle tremor.  

**Iron Stubbornness** 4-Track Trait
Dwarves are difficult to sway, intimidate, deceive, or magically influence. Gain +1d on actions where mental fortitude, steadfastness, or discipline matters. Once per session, you may ignore a mental compulsion, hallucinations, or mesmeric.

**Vengeful** 4-Track Trait
Dwarves are known to hold grudges. When making an action role against someone who has attacked, slighted, or been hostile to you or your allies, gain +1d.

**Craftsman** 3-Track Trait
Dwarves are known for their superior craftsmanship when it comes to metallurgy and stonework. When creating an item, add increased impact or two additional boxes to its track.

**Tied to Tradition** 3-Track Trait
Tradition runs deep as stone for dwarves. Mark to gain +2d to a skill you have no ranks in for the rest of the encounter.

### Orcs
___
**Orc Ferocity** 5-Track Trait
Orcs can keep going even when others could not. Remove 3 wounds when all wounds tracks are filled. You can only use this ability again after you have fully healed.

**Direct Honesty** 3-Track Trait
Deception is frowned upon in Orcish cultures - clear intent and plain speech solves problems. Gain +1d when negotiating, commanding, or persuading through telling the truth - even when it is blunt or uncomfortable. Once per session you may determine if someone is lying to you.

**Blood Before Steel** 3-Track Trait
An orc’s strength is inseparable from their people. When acting to protect, aid, or avenge an ally, gain +1 impact. If an ally were to take wounds, you may push yourself to take those wounds instead (if narrative allows it).

**Chiseled Charm** 4-Track Trait
Born with wonderfully set features, orcs can lead with charisma. Gain +1d vs those whom admire you. 

**Mired Past** 4-Track Trait
Orcs were often targeted with prejudice in the past, they have learned from past misgivings. When you push yourself, you may ignore one wound consequence. 

**Peak Physique** 1-Track Trait
Whether through large frame, force of will, or raw strength, orcs are accustomed with bearing burdens. Mark to increase your load by one category. 


### Halfling
___
**Undersized Advantage** 4-Track Trait
Halflings are blessed with agility and uncanny luck. Once per session, you may roll an action twice and take the better result.

**Opportunity Hunter** 4-Track Trait
Halflings always keep an eye out for a window of opportunity. When an opportunity opens unexpectedly - an ally creates a distraction, an enemy falters, or the environment shifts - you may immediately take a follow-up action with improved position.

**Living in Shadow** 3-Track Trait
Halflings have survived for centuries living in the shadow of other cultures, watching, listening, and adapting. Gain +1d when acting unnoticed, exploiting social hierarchies, navigating other cultures’ customs, or leveraging information gathered from the background. Once per job, you may declare that you know a useful custom, back channel, or informal contact.

*wip* **Practical Applications** 3-Track Trait
Simplicity is best, and you know how to make it last. When you take a long rest, you gain +1 marks to spend on any tracks you have.

**Kind** 3-Track Trait
You hate to see anything suffer. When assisting someone in dire need, desperate measure, or physical peril, take 1 less mire.

**Soul Food** 4-Track Trait
Good food is one of the best things in life. Gain +1d when food or drink is involved in a roll.

**Home in Time for Supper** 3-Trait Track
Known for their evasive nature, halflings can get out of many situations that others would deem unescapable. Gain improved position when attempting to maneuver out of a situation, whether physical or social.

**Rest is Best** 3-Trait Track


### Gnomes
___
**Down but Gnot Out** 5-Track Trait
Whether through sheer durability or shifty fey luck, Gnomes are hard to put down for good. 

**Sheer Curiosity** 4-Track Trait
Gnomes have a insatiable interest in everything around them. If someone uses a skill near you that you do not have, gain +1d to that skill until you pick another skill.

**Fey Foundling** 3-Track Trait
The blood of the Fey runs strong in you. Fey are friendlier to you on first contact. Fey magic has reduced impact on you.

**Trinket Collector** 3-Track Trait
Gnomes love to hoard all that glitters. Increase your load's limit by one. *Light: 4, Normal: 6, etc*.

**Gnomish Gizmos** 2-Track Trait
Many gnomes are skilled engineers, alchemists, tinkers, and inventors. When you create something, it takes 1 less load. A character may only carry 3 items made this way.

**Humbly Wrong** 3-Track Trait
Gnomes are willing to make mistakes and laugh at themselves in the process of perfecting what they do. After failing an action, gain +1d to the same action until you succeed.


## Supernatural Races
### Vampires
___
**Supernatural Healing** 3-Track Trait
Rolls made to heal an injury you're suffering from treat partial successes as full successes. Once per session you may remove 2 marks from mire when healing.

**Vampiric Sight** 3-Track Trait
Vampires can see in the dark with utmost clarity. Do not take penalties from darkness. You can push yourself to see weak points in mortal creatures.

**Dominate** 2-Track Trait
Whether attractive or intimidating, vampires have an uncanny ability to persuade others. Treat partial successes as full successes when attempting to get another to do your bidding.

**Heliophobia** 2-Track Trait
The sun severely weakens vampires. When doing any action in natural light take 1 Cut. Gain reduced harm to all but holy and silver effects.

**Children of the Night** 3-Track Trait
Some vampires have the ability to change shapes. Push yourself to change shape to a bat, wolf, mist or smoke. Exit the form at anytime. 

**Life Blood** 3-Track Trait
You are what you eat. Push yourself to consume a large amount of blood from a victim, healing in the process. 

**Predatory Poise** 3-Track Trait
Your body is unnaturally balanced and precise.  When you push yourself for physical actions, you may add +1d **and** +1 impact (only marking 2 mire).

**Blood-Bond** 4-Track Trait
You can forge a psychic or emotional tie with someone whose blood you have tasted. You may communicate with anyone you have bitten for the next 24-hours. You may aid them regardless of the distance.

**Hemomancy** 1-Track Trait
You do not take cut when blood is involved in your magic.

### Gargoyle
___
**Stone Skinned** 4-Track Trait
Your skin is as hard as stone. Once per session you may reduce harm taken from a single attack or mishap to 0 by pushing yourself.

**Vigilant Watch** 3-Track Trait
Gargoyles have acute senses and a natural predisposition for observation.  Gain +1d when surveying terrain, keeping watch, or detecting subtle movement or magical shifts. You cannot be surprised by environmental hazards or hidden intruders.

**Body of Stone** 3-Track Trait
Your stone body is resistant to extreme temperature and magical blight. Reduce consequences from environmental hazards (cold, heat, mist, magical decay) and gain +1d on rolls to stabilize or endure such conditions.

**Gothic Presence** 3-Track Trait
Your intimidating visage and unnatural posture unnerves others, often making them hesitate in crucial moments. Gain +1d on intimidation, commanding presence, or social roles where awe, fear, or respect matters. 

**Paradoxical Flight** 2-Track Trait
Though heavy as stone, some gargoyles have gained the ability to fly short distances. Push yourself to gain the ability to fly for a short duration.

**Stilled Silence** 3-Track Trait
Stilled Gargoyles are often mistaken for statues in the background. Treat partial successes as full successes when taking an action that benefits from staying still - hiding, listening, etc.

**Holy Ward** 2-Track Trait
Gargoyles are often used as protectors and wards from magics. Magics effects targeting you or near you have reduced impact. Also gain +1 impact when attempting to stop magical effects.

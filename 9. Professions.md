
## Enforcer
#### Starting Skills
- +2 **Skirmish** or **Wreck**
- +1 **Command** or **Sway**
#### Traits
##### Bodyguard 
4-Track
When you protect a teammate, you may make a skill check with 1 cut to reduce the wounds by that amount. When you gather info to anticipate possible threats in the current situation, you get +1 effect.
##### Mule
3-Track
Add +2 to your load limits; for instance Light: 5; Normal: 7; Heavy: 8;  
##### Hercules
2-Track
When you push yourself, you *display superhuman levels of strength,* and you do not take cut from Strength based actions.
##### Just a Scratch
3-Track
When you take the Heal Wounds [[11. Long Rests#Healing Wounds|long rest]] action, you heal half of your traits rounded up.
##### Warded 
1-Track
Magic affecting you takes -1 impact. When dispelling magics, you do not take cut.
